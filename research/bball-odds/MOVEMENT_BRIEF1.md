# Movement Brief #1 — NBA/NCAAB FG movement signals

Consensus = cross-book median. Trigger/grade = T-60 line & median price
(honest per grading framework; movement measured open→T-60, late = T-4h→T-60).
Breakeven at -110 = 52.4%.

## Conclusions (2026-07-16)

1. **Pure follow/fade movement does NOT clear the vig** in basketball FG
   markets at T-60 — every totals/spread bucket lands between -8% and +2%
   pooled. Same lesson as NFL: the market is efficient against naive steam.
2. **TRACKING ONLY (small n, not vault-worthy):** NBA small-dog (45-50% implied)
   ML steamed ≥2pp — home +18.5% (n=75), away +9.7% (n=97), but 22-23 was
   negative for both. NCAAB home med-dog steamed → +3.8% (n=263, 3/4 seasons
   positive). Revisit after 2026-27 adds sample.
3. NBA follow-steam turned positive in 24-25 + 25-26 across most buckets after
   losing in 22-23/23-24 — possible regime shift, not bettable yet.
4. Next: edges more likely in softer markets (1H, team totals, small-conf
   NCAAB) and in movement × OUR-model interactions once models exist.
