# Portal Brief #1 — roster continuity vs the market (NCAAB)

19,446 games. Continuity pct = within-season rank. T-60 prices. BE 52.4%.

## Conclusions (2026-07-16)

1. **The portal signal is real but ERA-DEPENDENT and more modest than the
   round-3 sleeper suggested.** Rebuilt AWAY teams Nov-Dec: 53.5%/+2.2%
   pooled — but -3/-4% in 22-23/23-24 vs **+7/+8% in 24-25/25-26**. The edge
   EMERGED as portal roster churn outran market re-rating. Portal-era only:
   ≈56.5%. Treat as an emerging, possibly decaying-alpha signal.
2. **The bettable version: rebuilt + EXPERIENCED transfers, away, Nov-Dec =
   57.8%/+10.3% (n=258).** Teams rebuilt with veteran transfers gel faster
   than the market prices; YOUNG rebuilds don't (51.7%). Mechanism-clean.
3. **The market catches up by January** — December is the peak month (54.0%),
   Jan+ is dead (50.2%). A calendar-bounded signal.
4. Rebuilt HOME teams: nothing (49.6%) — the effect is road-specific
   (public/books overweight "new roster on the road struggles" narrative).
5. Totals: nothing. Chaos games (both rebuilt): mildly positive ATS-away but
   era-driven; skip.

Status: TRACK-PLUS (bet small in 2026-27 Nov-Dec, re-validate era trend
before scaling; expect adaptation).
