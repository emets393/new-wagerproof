# H2H Matchup Brief #1 — does matchup history beat the close?

Prior meetings ≤730 days, both venues, market-relative (each meeting
measured vs its own T-60 line). Bets at T-60 consensus. Breakeven 52.4%.

## Conclusions (2026-07-16)

1. **NBA H2H ATS anti-persists**: teams that dominated a matchup ATS stop —
   backing the dominated HOME team next meeting = 55.5% / +5.9% (n=660, 3/4
   seasons; TRACK). Its mirror (ride the away dominator) loses -14.9%.
2. **NBA matchup total tendencies persist mildly**: prior meetings ran over
   by >=8 -> OVER = 53.1% / +1.4% (n=787, 3/4 seasons; TRACK) — pace-matchup
   style is real and slightly underweighted. Fading it is very wrong (-10.5%).
3. **NCAAB H2H carries nothing** beyond noise; under-tendencies persist at
   exactly breakeven.

Directional lesson for the product: matchup pages should emphasize H2H
totals tendencies (real, mildly predictive) over H2H ATS records (mean-
reverting in NBA, noise in CBB).
