# KenPom Brief #1 тАФ fanmatch model edge vs market (NCAAB)

22,712 games with KenPom predictions joined. All bets at T-60 line/price.
spread_edge>0 = KenPom likes HOME vs the line; total_edge>0 = KenPom leans OVER.

## Conclusions (2026-07-16)

**The T-60 closing market fully prices KenPom.** No spread- or total-edge
bucket clears the vig (all -10% to -2% pooled), and neither steam-confirmation
nor buy-the-dip interactions rescue it. Bigger KP edges actually do WORSE тАФ
when KenPom disagrees hard with the close, the market is right.

Implication for our own NCAAB model (step 2): matching KenPom is worth
nothing. The model must add information KenPom doesn't carry (per-game
boxscore shapes, schedule spots, injuries/roster, small-conf coverage) тАФ
and softer markets (1H, team totals) are the better first target.
