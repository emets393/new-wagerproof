1. **"Fade ranked road teams" was real and the market fixed it**: 53.8%/+2.7%
   pooled but 60%→55%→47%→52% by season — dead since 2024-25. The nonconf
   version was huge (fade won ~59-64% in 22-24) and equally decayed. DECAYED.
2. **Streak interactions are priced.** The exact hot-dog-vs-cold-favorite
   scenario: home version 54.0%/+3.1% (n=150, inconsistent), away version
   negative. Slight lean: in streak-mismatch games the HOME side outperforms
   (back cold home vs hot away 53.5%/+2.1%) — sub-breakeven, noted only.
3. **Meeting-2 details priced**; same-venue rematch winner repeats 57.4%/+9.6%
   (n=209) but 22-23-driven (72%→51-55%). "They won there last time" upset
   repeat = over-bet (47.7% backing the upset winner again).
4. All consistent with the settled thesis: situational/relational angles are
   priced; personnel news is not.
